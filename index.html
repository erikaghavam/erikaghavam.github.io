About me


I'm Erika, and I'm a

Hello! My name is Erika Ghavampouri and my mission is to make sure that as many students as possible have access to good quality English learning education, to achieve new levels of success, and to reach their full potential. As a private teacher, I believe that all students learn differently, which is why I tailor make each individual lesson and plan with the learner's benefit in mind, to maximise their engagement and language acquisition, through games, learning activities and conversation. English is a diverse language, covering a wide range of fields and interests, and so am I; with my background of sport, art, and the humanities, I am able to demonstrate and engage students in a variety of topics, to ensure that they come out as well-rounded, confident speakers. Being a native speaker of English allows me to understand the challenges and needs for the English language, and provide insights to all my students. I hope that by creating such lessons, that I can actively contribute to helping one learn a language they can use for life.


As a teacher at Maha Talk, I was responsible for teaching English as a second language to children of varying ages. By applying a dynamic approach to learning whilst combining with fun activities and a student-to-student individual understanding, I have been delivering highly engaging activities for almost a year, giving me experience in different facets of English learning, from teaching the very basics to upper-intermediate content, delivering a suite of English language learning platforms - from business to beginners English, from conversational to contextual.
